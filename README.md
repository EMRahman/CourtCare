# Idea for Ball Kids – Health & Logistics Assist

A hidden wristband worn under the clothing wrist bands that serves two purposes: health tracking and match logistics.

---

## 1. Health Tracking

Monitors heart rate, temperature, calories burned, and movement/energy used so supervisors get a full picture and can receive an alert before a ball person faints.

**Testing:** Can be trialled during circuit training to review which children are exerting the most energy, as well as providing health metrics for identifying optimal/high performers who can endure longer shifts on court.

## 2. Haptic Vibrations

Linked to the live scoring system so ball kids always know what's coming:

| Buzz | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| 1 buzz | Balls may change ends if next point is won | Receiver wins at 15–40 |
| 2 buzzes | Balls will definitely change ends next | 6 points into a tie break |
| 3 buzzes | New balls when game ends | After first 7 then every 9 games |
| 4 buzzes | Match point | Be ready to stand at the front for ball kids at the net |

**Note:** This assists ball kids in mentally tracking games — but they should still track independently in case the logic is wrong.

---

## Rollout

- **Year 1** — Deploy on smaller outside courts for testing. Health tracking introduced first as it's passive and safety-critical.
- **Year 2** — Expand to all courts. Layer in haptic vibrations once the tech is proven.

## Why It Works

- **Invisible** — No screen, no branding, hidden under the clothing wrist band. Completely avoids the  sponsorship issue.
- **Duty of care** — Protecting minors makes it very difficult for anyone to push back.

Grand slam gets to lead the sport on player welfare **and** improve match operations.
